# Behandlingsteknikker - Kodeoversikt

## MANIPULASJON/MOBILISERING

### Columna
- **C0, C1, C2...C7**: Cervical vertebrae
  - Retninger: fra hø/ve, i rotasjon, +z-y, PR (posterior-right), PL (posterior-left)
- **T1-T12**: Thoracic vertebrae
  - Retninger: post (posterior), PB (posterior-bilateral), fra hø/ve
- **L1-L5**: Lumbar vertebrae
  - Retninger: PL, PR, stående
- **P-R**: Posterior-to-Right sacrum/ilium
  - Varianter: P-R ISD, P-R fs, P-R ss, P-R kick, P-R drop
- **P-L**: Posterior-to-Left sacrum/ilium
  - Varianter: P-L fs, P-L ss, P-L ISD, P-L kick

### Ribbein (Ribs)
- **1-12 ribbe hø/ve**: Rib adjustments
  - Teknikker: DNG (Direct Non-force Technique), supine, prone

### Ekstremiteter
- **hø/ve ben fs**: Hip flexion-sidebending
- **hø/ve ben ss**: Hip sidebending-sidebending
- **hø/ve ankel fs/ss**: Ankle mobilization
- **Drop hø/ve IS ledd**: Drop technique for SI joint
- **Drop hø/ve hofte**: Drop technique for hip
- **PI/EX ilium**: Posterior/Exterior ilium correction

### Skulder
- **hø/ve gh**: Glenohumeral joint
  - Retninger: post (posterior), ant (anterior)
- **hø/ve scapula**: Scapula mobilization
  - Varianter: supine, prone, mm (muscles)
- **SC/GH**: Sternoclavicular/Glenohumeral
- **fs ant/post arm**: Flexion-sidebending anterior/posterior arm
- **ss arm**: Sidebending-sidebending arm

## BLØTVEVSBEHANDLING

### Massasje
- **massasje [område]**: General soft tissue work
- **Gun**: Massage gun/percussion therapy
- **TB**: Trykkbølge (Shockwave) or Trigger point
- **Scrape/graston**: Instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization

### Spesifikke Teknikker
- **PNF [muskel/område]**: Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation
  - Eksempler: pnf hø hofte, pnf ve pec, pnf nakke
- **ART [muskel]**: Active Release Technique
  - Eksempler: ART ve psoas, ART hø scap mm
- **ORM**: Ocular Range of Motion work
- **IMS**: Intramuscular Stimulation (dry needling)

### Spesifikke Strukturer
- **Occiput**: Suboccipital release
- **Subocc**: Suboccipital muscles
- **SCM**: Sternocleidomastoid
- **Traps**: Trapezius (øvre/nedre/mid)
- **ES**: Erector spinae
- **QL**: Quadratus lumborum
- **Pec**: Pectoralis (major/minor)
- **Scap mm**: Scapular muscles
- **Rhomb**: Rhomboids
- **Lev scap**: Levator scapulae
- **Psoas**: Iliopsoas
- **Piri**: Piriformis
- **Glut**: Gluteus (max/med/min)
- **TFL**: Tensor fasciae latae
- **Hamstring**: Hamstring muscles
- **Quad**: Quadriceps
- **Gastro**: Gastrocnemius
- **Soleus**: Soleus

## VESTIBULÆR/NEVROLOGISK BEHANDLING

### Manøvre
- **Epley**: For BPPV (specify kanal: ve/hø bakre/horisontale)
- **BBQ roll**: For horizontal canal BPPV
- **Brandt-Daroff**: Habituation exercises

### Øvelser (noteres som gitt)
- **ssep [side]**: Smooth pursuit exercises
- **8 tall [side]**: Figure-8 exercises
- **halm [side] + opk [side]**: Visual exercises
- **sakkader**: Saccade exercises (opp/ned/hø/ve)
- **Times 0 [retning]**: Circle exercises
- **Gaze**: Gaze stabilization
- **VOR**: Vestibulo-ocular reflex training
- **Sirkler**: Circle exercises (hø/ve)
- **KD**: Convergence-divergence

### Spesialisert
- **Øret/ører**: Ear drainage/manipulation
- **Traction**: Cervical traction
- **Vibrasjon**: Vibration technique

## TAPE OG HJELPEMIDLER
- **Tape [område]**: Kinesiotaping
- **Kinesiotape**: Specific kinesiology taping
- **Strikk øvelser**: Resistance band exercises

## FORKORTELSER I BEHANDLING
- **Beh**: Behandling
- **som sist**: Same as last treatment
- **ua**: Uten anmerkning (unremarkable)
- **neg**: Negative
- **pos**: Positive
- **bilat/bl**: Bilateral
- **ant**: Anterior
- **post**: Posterior
- **lat**: Lateral
- **med**: Medial
- **sup**: Superior
- **inf**: Inferior
- **hø**: Høyre (right)
- **ve**: Venstre (left)
- **mm**: Muscles/millimeter (context dependent)
